This first thing that im going to explore is the EDM (Electronic Dance Music) scene while im relatively new to this music, I've been listening for a little over three years now. I have some stuff to say about it, this music literally has something for everyone whether you want something chill to study or fall asleep to, or you want to moshpit and put your body on the line, the broad genre has something for everybody. Not only is the music a great reason to start listening, but the community is even more of a reason, they provide a caring and judgement free place for anyone who wants to be involved. Living by the policy of PLUR (Peace,love,unity, respect) this is the one of two rules, the other being be true to yourself. Still not convinced then let me cap my rant off with talking about the concerts and festivals, for every concert the production value is insane, it is always worth every penny. Festivals are on another level, do you ever wish you could recapture the worder that you had as a child, if see go to a EDM festival you will not be dissapointed.
