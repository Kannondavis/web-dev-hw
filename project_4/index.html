Hi, I am Kannon and welcome to my stuff. The intent of this site is to give you a doorway into my mind, so you can see what I like, what I don't, and other weird things that just spark my interest. Be sure to check out all the pages as it is experience meant to be seen start to finish.

The current homepage highlight is summer beers, with the weather finally getting hotter it is time for refreshing beers. What I like in a summer beer is something with strong citrus notes, a bit or tartness, and something that still taste good even when the sun is starting to warm it up. Here are my recommendations Draught Works - Pineapple express, KettleHouse - Fish On!, and Big Sky Brewing - Summer Honey. Every one of these beers is great and deserves a taste.
